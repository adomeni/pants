Step 6: Waistband

So we have pants, but we can’t stop here. Let’s put on the waistband.

Hopefully you cut the waistband on the fold of the fabric because that’s the easiest method to work with. What you need to do now is fold each end of the waistband to your desired seam allowance (typically 5/8”) and pin the waistband to the pants with right sides together. Sew along this seam then fold the other long edge of the waistband up to your seam allowance. Iron the first seam flat then, fold the waistband to the inside of the pants and pin it in place, following the front seam of the waistband. Now topstitch the inside of the waistband to the inside of the pants and sew along the rest of the perimeter of the waistband. Follow the edges as close as possible if you want the waistband to look professional!
