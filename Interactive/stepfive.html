Step 5: Attaching Front and Back Pants

This is the most exciting part about sewing pants. In a few steps you’ll be able to finally try on your pants and admire the work you’ve done! The proper way to attach the front and back is by the side seams first, then the inseam. So go ahead! What are you waiting for?

Side Seams

Start at the top, and pin the front to the back with right sides together. Line up all of the notches that were cut out previously. After pinning, sew each leg individually from the top to the bottom of the leg.

Inseam

For the inseam, pin the center front and center back together at the bottom of the crotch with right sides facing each other. Start at the end of one leg and stitch all the way up and down the other leg. Iron the seam flat.

NOW you can try on your pants and appreciate all of the hard work!
