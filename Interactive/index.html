Hello and welcome to the wonderful world of sewing! If you’ve ever wanted to learn how to make your very own pants, you’ve come to the right place. If not, please enjoy this tutorial anyway! I will take you on a wild step-by-step journey through my process of sewing some trusty pants. By the end, you will hopefully be an expert. We will focus on using a self-made pants pattern but do not worry, it is not as hard as it seems!

Here’s a list of things you’ll definitely need:

  • Sewing Machine
  • Thread
  • 2 Metres of fabric
  • Sewing needle
  • Pattern
  • Zipper
  • Buttons
  • Pins
  • Scissors
  • Iron
  • Seam Ripper
  • Old pair of Jeans
